Children's Nutritional Status Monitoring System for the Barangay

A Web-based software for A Better Monitoring of Every Children's Health Status
   This website system is a system that may help nutrition or a barangay health scholar to monitor the status of every children in their respective area. Using our system, it can easily update every data and monitor the changing of the weight of every child. The system also has a food and nutrition tab that have a recommendation food to guide a mother to choose what nutrition food they will prepare for their child. It’s also has plan and programs tab that post the government’s programs that gives proper information to the parents, health activities and announcements.
   This system was composed of different programming language such as Hypertext Markup Language (HTML) for the function, Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) for the design, Java Script (JS) to make the web page more interactive, and PHP for the database.
